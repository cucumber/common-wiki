Communication within a team always works better when people can use their spoken language. Cucumber supports "several spoken languages":../tree/master/lib/cucumber/languages.yml. This is Norwegian:

<pre><code>Egenskap: Summering
  For å slippe å gjøre dumme feil
  Som en regnskapsfører
  Vil jeg kunne legge sammen

  Scenario: to tall
    Gitt at jeg har tastet inn 5
    Og at jeg har tastet inn 7
    Når jeg summerer
    Så skal resultatet være 12

  Scenario: tre tall
    Gitt at jeg har tastet inn 5
    Og at jeg har tastet inn 7
    Og at jeg har tastet inn 1
    Når jeg summerer
    Så skal resultatet være 13
</code></pre>

You have to tell Cucumber that you're using a different language than English (the default):
<pre><code>cucumber --language no</code></pre>

h2. Adding a new language

It's easy! 

# Make a fork of Cucumber and pull it down
# Add your language's keywords to  "languages.yml":../tree/master/lib/cucumber/languages.yml
# Generate the parser for your language:
<pre><code>rake treetop:compile</code></pre>

That's it! You have a new language that you can pass to <pre><code>--language</code></pre>
