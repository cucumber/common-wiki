h2. Ruby on Rails

As of Cucumber 0.5.0 you should also install/upgrade the <code>cucumber-rails</code> gem. After doing that you must also run <code>ruby script/generate cucumber</code>. See cucumber-rails' "History.txt":http://github.com/aslakhellesoy/cucumber-rails/blob/master/History.txt for details.
